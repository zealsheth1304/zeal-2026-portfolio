# Description

<div className="text-ds-b1 my-8"> 
For this project, I created the virtual real-time social space that has a casual vibe. This space can be used to explore trivia about different destination locations. It is like a virtual road trip. Every room is a different destination from rainforests to snow-clad mountains to desserts. Users chose to go different rooms depending on their mood. Users can interact with other users in the room and can play few games there.

# My Learnings

Learnt the key factors affecting the design of real-time social spaces. Understood the importance of leveraging the advantages of being in virtual space rather than trying to imitate the physical world interaction as there are different sensory inputs and outputs at play for the two spaces.
