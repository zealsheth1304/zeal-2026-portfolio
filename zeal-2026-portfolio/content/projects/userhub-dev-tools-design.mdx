# Overview

Userhub is a SaaS platform that helps startups and growth-stage companies manage users, 
pricing plans, and subscriptions. The team noticed friction points in role management, 
onboarding, and how value propositions were communicated on the marketing site. 
I partnered with product and engineering to improve these experiences, 
focusing on **reducing roles’ assignment support overhead, improving onboarding 
completion, and clarifying the product’s value for potential customers.**

# The Problem


<p>Based on the feedback from multiple stakeholders, I identified the following pain points:</p>

<div className="flex flex-col gap-8 items-start bg-slate-50 dark:bg-slate-900/50 p-8 rounded-2xl border border-border-strong/50 my-12 mb-16">
  <div className="flex-1">
    <h3 className="text-main font-bold mb-8">My Research Touchpoints</h3>
    <ul className="text-ds-body-2 list-disc pl-5 space-y-4">
    <li>**Roles Management:** The existing admin console made assigning and editing roles unintuitive, which generated repeated support tickets and slowed down adoption.</li>
    <li>**Impersonation Workflow:** The impersonation workflow had confusing copy and inconsistent patterns, leading to onboarding drop-offs and frustration.</li>
    <li>**Marketing Website:** The marketing website was not clear on the product’s value proposition and was not optimized for conversion.</li> 
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

# The Solution

<div className="flex-1">
<p className="text-ds-b1 text-primary font-semibold font-sans mb-4">To design effective solutions, I quickly
 ramped up on how APIs and webhooks functioned behind the scenes so that I could write accurate, 
 context-aware copy for the admin experience. </p>
<p className="text-ds-b1 text-main font-bold font-sans mb-8">I conducted deep research into best practices for developer-facing tools, closely studying leading
 platforms like Stripe, Maxio, Zuora, Paddle, WorkOS, and Auth0. This helped me understand industry
  standards and identify opportunities where Userhub could stand out.</p>
</div>

<div className="flex flex-col gap-8 items-start bg-slate-50 dark:bg-slate-900/50 p-8 rounded-2xl border border-border-strong/50 my-12 mb-16">
  <div className="flex-1">
    <h3 className="text-main font-bold mb-8">1. Roles Management Redesign</h3>
    <ul className="text-ds-body-2 list-disc pl-5 space-y-4">
    <li>**Problem:** Admins got confused in assigning roles/permissions.</li>
    <li>**Solution:** I designed a new roles management interface with simplified hierarchy of roles, clearer affordances for assignment, and contextual helper text at every step.</li>
    <li>**Impact:** Admins assign roles with fewer errors and faster.</li> 
    </ul>
    <CaseStudyImage src="/assets/Userhub/RoleModalRedesign.webp" label="Role Modal Redesign" className="my-12" />
  </div>

<div className="flex flex-col gap-8 items-start bg-slate-50 dark:bg-slate-900/50 p-8 rounded-2xl border border-border-strong/50 my-12 mb-16">

    <ul className="text-ds-body-2 list-disc pl-5 space-y-4">
    <li>**Problem:** Admins didn’t feel confident they were viewing the right user environment.</li>
    <li>**Solution:** I designed a new impersonation workflow with clearer labels and confirmation states. For sign-in, rewrote error messages in plain language and aligned UI with the product’s system design.</li>
