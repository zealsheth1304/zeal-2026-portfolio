# Situation
*The context and the high-stakes problem space.*

In early 2024, the luxury automotive market was facing a digital engagement plateau. Standard product pages were static, failing to evoke the prestige and visceral thrill of being behind the wheel. We needed to create a digital flagship that matched the brand's physical excellence.

**The Problem**: Engagement rates for high-intent visitors were dropping by 15% due to a lack of interactive storytelling.

# Task
*The specific mission and professional accountability.*

As the Lead Product Designer, my goal was to bridge the gap between technical precision and emotional luxury. I was tasked with designing an interactive 3D configurator that wouldn't just "show" features, but would curate an experience.

**Key Objectives**:
1. Increase "Book a Test Drive" conversions by 20%.
2. Implement a seamless 3D transition system.
3. Ensure 100% brand alignment with the new "Prestige" design language.

<ImagePlaceholder label="Strategy Deck & Goal Setting" />

# Action
*The strategic process, iterations, and problem-solving steps.*

### 1. Research & Synthesis
I conducted deep-dive interviews with 15 UHNW (Ultra-High-Net-Worth) individuals to understand their digital expectations. The common theme was **"Effortless Control."**

### 2. Design Iterations
We moved from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, testing the 4px grid system we built for the design system.

<VideoPlaceholder label="Configurator Interaction Prototype" />

### 3. Technical Implementation
Working closely with the engineering team, we optimized Three.js models to ensure smooth performance on mobile—crucial for 60% of our user base.

<ImagePlaceholder label="Technical Architecture & Optimization" />

# Result
*The data-driven impact and key takeaways.*

The launch was a defining moment for the brand's digital presence. We didn't just meet our goals; we redefined the standard for automotive e-commerce.

- **+25% Conversion**: Exceeded the test drive booking goal within the first month.
- **92% Positive Sentiment**: Based on post-interaction feedback loops.
- **Award Winning**: Featured in multiple design galleries for technical excellence.

<ImagePlaceholder label="Final High-Fidelity Presentation" />

### Key Learnings
Designing for luxury isn't about adding "more"—it's about adding **intentionality**. Every 4px of spacing and every serif font choice was a strategic decision to build trust and prestige.
