<p>I conducted design research to gain clarity on the **content design, table interaction design and error handling** for Stackup Editor.</p>

pro-user mostly, we assumed users know what they are doing, we decided to go 
ahead with design **Option 3** for glitch-free user experience, fast shipping  and 
complete user freedom.
</div>
