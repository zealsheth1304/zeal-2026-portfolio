# Overview

Instacart is an American grocery delivery and pick-up service in the USA and Canada. During
the pandemic the online ordering behavior increased drastically. Instacart tried to capitalize
the opportunity with new delivery models and ad campaigns. But when I used website, I found it
exhausting. This inspired me to carry out a full-blown research to investigate user needs and 
frustrations while shopping groceries online on Instacart website.

<div className="text-ds-body-1 text-primary font-semibold font-sans my-8">
Based on the insights, I redesigned the Instacart's homepage layout to search items more intuitively through navigation and AI-based predictions to help users shop faster and efficiently with least friction.

# Impact & Learnings



<div className="flex flex-col gap-8 items-start bg-slate-50 dark:bg-slate-900/50 p-8 rounded-2xl border border-border-subtle px-8 py-4 my-12">
  <div className="flex-1 pt-8">
  **Expected outcome:** Increase in the customer (employers) conversion rate because of step-by-step onboarding, clarity 
in terminology and displaying performance statistics on website to persuade, reduced number of clicks to find active candidates.
    <div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-x-12 gap-y-12 my-12">
      <div className="flex flex-col items-start gap-6 ">
        <span className="text-6xl w-32 text-primary font-serif font-bold tracking-tight">75%</span>
        <span className="text-ds-b1 font-sans text-main">Reduction in time to find items (120sec → 30sec ) to add to cart, due to,‘Optimized Search’ options, repeated shopping behavior predictibility.</span>
      </div>
      <div className="flex flex-col items-start gap-6">
        <span className="text-6xl w-32 text-primary font-serif font-bold tracking-tight">2.5X</span>
        <span className="text-ds-b1 font-sans text-main">Increase in user’s willingness to buy fresh produce (2 users → 5 users of 6), due to,‘Shelf Life Information’ for fresh produce items on the website.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>



<div className="text-ds-b1 text-primary font-semibold font-sans mb-8">
  My Learnings: Users rely on ‘Search‘ button much more than Navigation. New features can be successfully implemented only if they have familiarity less cognitive effort.
</div>
