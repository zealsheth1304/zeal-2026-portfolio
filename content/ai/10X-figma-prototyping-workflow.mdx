        <li>Precision Prompting: I used Figma Make AI by providing specific "foundation blocks"—detailing Content, Interaction, and Visual Aesthetics rather than asking for a generic layout.</li>

        <li>Hybrid Refinement: I partnered with Claude Code for direct edits and performed manual Figma fixes to polish the final UI.</li>

# Important Tips

<div className="text-ds-body-1 font-sans text-main mt-8">
    <p>To ensure website is SEO friendly, accessible, easily modifiable and scalable, you can implement specific Logic Rules and Skills:</p>   
</div>

<div className="text-ds-body-1 font-sans text-main my-8">
    <ul className="mt-4">
        <li><strong>Context is King:</strong> AI is only as sophisticated as your prompt. Specify your inputs, expected outputs, and constraints clearly.</li>
        <li><strong>Don't Outsource Your Eyes:</strong> Always run a heuristic check on AI outputs. AI can handle the "labor," but you must handle the "logic."</li>
        <li><strong>Feed the System:</strong> Provide your design system or brand guidelines manually. It saves tokens and prevents hallucinated styles.</li>
        <li><strong>Iterate, Don't Just Regenerate:</strong> Use AI to fix specific parts of a design rather than asking for a total redo every time.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
