# Detailed Process
<div className="text-ds-body-1 font-sans text-main mt-8">
    <p className="mb-2"><span className="font-semibold">1. The Current State UX Audit</span> (Roleplaying as a Marketing Ad Campaign Designer for a Sunglasses company):</p>
    <p className="mb-12">I began by diving into Google Flow, creating a 30-second video ad with a rough script to experience its current capabilities firsthand and conduct a thorough UX audit.</p>
    <p className="mb-2"><span className="font-semibold">2. Background Research: Top-down &amp; Bottom-up Approaches</span></p>
    <p className="mb-2">My core question: 'How might we enhance generative AI video editing capabilities for creatives with Google Flow?' I combined:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6 mb-4 space-y-1">
        <li><strong>Top-down:</strong> Extensive research on real user needs across filmmaking, marketing, animation, VFX, and virtual production.</li>
        <li><strong>Bottom-up:</strong> Identifying functions achievable with current AI models like Image Segmentation, LLMs, Generative AI Image Creation, 3D Object AI Generation, and advanced motion animation (Genie Model, Gemini Robotics).</li>
    </ul>
    <p className="mb-12">By mapping these, I found the intersection of viable features that create real user impact.</p>
    <p className="mb-2"><span className="font-semibold">3. AI-Native Product UX Design — Redesign Highlights:</span></p>
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6 mb-4 space-y-1">
        <li><strong>Object Recognition &amp; Tweaking:</strong> Imagine effortlessly isolating and manipulating elements within your video with AI precision.</li>
        <li><strong>Multimodal, Intuitive Interaction:</strong> Human-like interactions (doodling, select area, pointing objects) for seamless control and utilization of generative AI tools.</li>
        <li><strong>Error Handling with Implicit &amp; Explicit Feedback:</strong> Intelligent systems that guide users and learn from their actions.</li>
        <li><strong>AI Guardrails &amp; Bad Actors Check:</strong> Robust mechanisms for identifying and mitigating offensive, age-restricted, sensitive, or harmful content.</li>
        <li><strong>Design for Sustainable AI Usage (Planning Mode):</strong> 'Planning Phase' to address heavy token usage, providing options for iterations and refine outcomes before high-resolution rendering, significantly reducing AI token expenditure.</li>
        <li><strong>Privacy &amp; Security:</strong> Multi-factor consent forms and advanced deepfake prevention.</li>
        <li><strong>Credibility &amp; Plagiarism-Combat:</strong> AI fingerprinting, watermarking, and co-creation badges to ensure authenticity and attribution.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
