# Overview

As Eventz.ai app scaled, the product team faced design inconsistencies, 
repetitive workflows, and technical debt. There was no single source of truth
for design or engineering, making it hard to deliver cohesive experiences.

<div className="text-ds-body-1 font-semibold font-sans text-main mt-8">
To establish a unified design language, I single-handedly spearheaded the 
end-to-end development of a scalable design system for the AI interface 
that would accelerate workflows and ensure a cohesive, and accessible user 
experience.
